Automotive Engineering Challenge

Design the future of automotive technology

Event Description

The Automotive Engineering Challenge invites teams to innovate in automotive design and technology. Develop solutions for electric vehicles, autonomous systems, or performance optimization. Showcase your expertise in vehicle dynamics, powertrain systems, and automotive electronics.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team size: 3-4 members
  • Duration: 2 days
  • Use CAD software for design
  • Include simulation results
  • Consider safety regulations
  • Present working prototype/simulation
  • Submit technical documentation
