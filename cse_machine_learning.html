DHEEMI SAVARI

Slow bike Race

Event Description

To ride a motorcycle or scooter as slowly as possible without touching the ground, stalling, or losing balance. The last rider to cross the finish line without breaking any rules is the winner.

Team size:4 players per team

Fees:100/- per team

Rules & Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Riders must have a valid motorcycle license and sign a safety waiver.
  • Motorcycle Type: Boys must use geared motorcycles, and girls must use non-geared scooters; no modifications allowed, and motorcycles must pass a safety check.
  • Motorcycle Type: Boys must use geared motorcycles, and girls must use non-geared scooters; no modifications allowed, and motorcycles must pass a safety check.
  • Track Specifications: A 40-50m straight or marked track with 1.5-2m wide lanes; leaving the lane results in disqualification.
  • Rider Behavior: Hands must remain on handlebars, feet on footrests, and no swerving, stunts, reversing, or interference with others.
  • Disqualification: Foot touching the ground, lane crossing, stalling for 3+ seconds, reckless behavior, removing hands from handlebars, intentional contact, or missing safety gear.
  • Present your solution to the judges
  • Code must be properly versioned using Git

Race Procedure:

  • Start: Riders line up at the start, and on signal (flag, buzzer, or whistle), they move forward as slowly as possible without stopping or touching the ground.
  • During the Race: Judges monitor the track; riders must maintain a steady pace and stay within their lane, with sudden stops or jerky movements penalized.
  • Finish: The last rider to legally cross the finish line wins; if all are disqualified, the rider who lasted the longest without violations is the winner.

Safety Regulations:

  • Full-face helmet (mandatory).
  • Riding gloves, jacket, and shoes (strongly recommended).
  • No alcohol, drugs, or unsafe condition riders allowed
  • First aid personnel and emergency support on-site.
