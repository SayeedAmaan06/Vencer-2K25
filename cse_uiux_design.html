KAKSHA CRICKET

Classroom Cricket

Event Description

Classroom Cricket is a short-format cricket game designed to encourage teamwork and fun among students. The game consists of mixed-gender teams playing a quick and exciting 4-over match, ensuring fair participation and balanced competition

Team size:5 players per team (3 boys and 2 girls.)

Fees:300/- per team

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Composition: Each team consists of 5 players (3 boys, 2 girls) and must set their batting and bowling order before the match.
  • Match Format: Each innings lasts 4 overs, with both teams batting and bowling once; the team with the most runs wins.
  • Powerplay Over: One pre-decided over doubles all runs scored.
  • Bowling Restrictions: Girls must bowl alternate overs, and each bowler can bowl only one over per innings.
  • Scoring System: 4 runs for grounded boundaries, 6 runs for aerial shots (girls only); if a boy hits a six, he is out. Powerplay doubles all runs.
  • Fair Play: Matches must be played respectfully, and the umpire's decision is final.
