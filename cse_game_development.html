BATTLE GROUNDS MOBILE INDIA

Kill for Cash.

Event Description

Event Overview: Join us for an action-packed BGMI event at AITM to Show off your skills, compete with top players, and battle your way to victory. Exciting prizes and bragging rights await. Don't miss the ultimate gaming showdown.

Team size: 4 members per team.

Fees:400 per team.

Tournament Format:

  • Match Type: All matches are played in Squad Mode (TPP).
  • Tournament Structure: Knockout rounds determine the top teams, who then compete in the final stage.
  • Maps & Game Mode: Matches will be played on Erangel, Miramar, and Sanhok, with the number of maps based on team count.

Point System:

  • Placement Points: 1st - 10, 2nd - 6, 3rd - 5, 4th - 4, 5th - 3, 6th - 2, 7th & 8th - 1.
  • Kill Points: 1 Kill = 1 Point.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Only students from the college or invited colleges can participate, registering with their real name, college ID, and IGN; smurf or multiple accounts are not allowed.
  • Team Composition: Each team must have 4 main players, with substitutes allowed only before a match starts.
  • Game Rules: Players must use the latest official BGMI version on mobile devices only (no emulators, tablets, or controllers).
  • Lobby & Start Time: Teams must join 10 minutes before the match; late teams will be left out.
  • Device & Network Issues: No pauses for technical issues; disconnected players can rejoin from where they left off.
  • Cheating & Violations: Hacks, scripts, emulators, or toxic behavior result in immediate disqualification.
  • Spectators & Streaming: Only official casters/referees can spectate; players need permission to stream.
