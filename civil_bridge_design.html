Round to Quest.

Event Description

"Round to Stuck" describes a situation where a game, especially one with round-based gameplay (like a game with multiple stages), where players have to clear each rounds to unlock next round.

Registration fees: 300 /-per team

Rules & Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Open to all engineering students; participants must carry their college ID.
  • Team Composition: Each team must have 4 members (2 boys & 2 girls).
  • Game Format: The event consists of multiple rounds.
  • Materials: All required materials will be provided by the organizing team.
  • Progression: Teams must clear each round within the stipulated time to advance to the next.
  • Winning Criteria: The team that completes all rounds within the time limit wins.
