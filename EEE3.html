Event Description

Team size: 2 members in a team

Registration Fees: 200 rupees

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Abstract should not exceed 250 words and Paper should not exceed 5 pages.
  • Bring your college I-Card & receipt on event day. All teams and their respective members should reach the event place in time.
  • Please bring your power point presentation on a pen drive.
  • Please bring 3 hard copies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
  • Paper and Power Point Presentation should be in 97- 2007 format.
  • The teams will get 10+2 minutes for presentation followed by a question- and-answer session.
  • Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
