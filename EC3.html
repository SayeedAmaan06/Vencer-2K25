WHISTERS OF THE VAULT

Event Description

1. Paper Pursuit Round

2. Location Quest Round

3. Riddle Quest Round

4. Final Pursuit

Registration Fees: 100 rupees

Date: 8th & 9th April 2025

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Game should be played in a group of 2 to 4 members.
  • Attendance of all the group members is mandatory.
  • Participants receive a series of clues, which can be in the form of riddles, puzzles, or instructions, leading them to the next location or item.
  • No tempering with clues, misguiding other team, or using external help unless allowed.
  • Not to touch the clues of other teams, should have to focus on respective team clues.
  • Totally there will be 5 flags that the team have to collect it, at the end of the game the team should have all the 5 flags, missing of 1 flag can make the team disqualify.
  • The team that finds the final treasure first and completes all tasks will win.
